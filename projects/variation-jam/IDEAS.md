# IDEAS

## Basic Tag

Two dots, one the player, the other an NPC.
One of the dots starts as "it", with "it" displayed above its position.
Touching another dot gives it the "it".
A timer. At the end of the time, if you aren't "it" you win.

## Submarine Tag

Multiple dots, one "it", the rest NPCs.
A tag that touches a non-tagged character freezes it, until another
dot touches the frozen character to free it.
Games ends after time elapses or the tag freezes all of the moving dots.

## Ball Tag

Basic tag with a twist: Tag other characters by throwing a ball at them.
If you miss you must go grab the ball again.
Game ends after time elapses.

## Magnetic Tag

Basic tag, but you are magnets with the same polarity, so you can never reach
the other characters... unless you use their magnetism against them!

## Typing Tag

Type faster than the other characters to reach their position.
If you reach their position, you have now to type backwards.
If you reach the start before they do, you win.
If the time elapses whoever is it loses.

## Safe Zone Tag

Reach the Safe Zone while avoiding other tags.
If you do you win. If you get touched, you lose.

## Underwater Tag

Super Mario water level physics.
Basic tag rules apply.

## Space Tag

Floaty physics.
Basic tag rules apply.

## Aggregator Tag

Aggregate all of the other characters to win.
But you get slower as you aggregate more and more characters.
You can use the mouse to move aggregated characters to make different shapes to ease your game.
Zombie tag rules.

## The Game Tag

An Easter Egg.
A variant of Zombie Tag where just by displaying the Wikipedia page
of The Game meme, you are also tagged. You lose just by playing.

## The Clock Tag

You are the seconds hand of the clock.
When your hand touches another hand, they are it.
You have 30 seconds to reach the other hand, but the clock moves at normal speed.
Time your game properly!

## The Realistic Tag

A simple text display telling you that you are it, and to touch another person in your vicinity.
If you do, you can come back and press space to win.
A timer. At the end of the time, if you haven't pressed space, you lose.

## The Phone Tag

Call a friend to tell them they are it!
No touch-backsies!

## The Minotaur Tag

Tag, but in a maze. Exit the maze without being touched to win.

## PacMan Tag

Tag, but you are PacMan and must eat all of the dots to win.
Phantoms become faster and more intelligent the longer you spend player.
